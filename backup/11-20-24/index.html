About All Valley Urgent Care

All Valley Urgent Care is a quality urgent care facility in Brawley and El Centro, California, where skilled specialists care for patients of all ages in a friendly, comfortable setting.

All Valley Urgent Care takes an integrative, cutting-edge approach to caring for patients, offering same-day, walk-in, and telehealth visits. The urgent care providers understand that patients are busy, which is why they make exceptional health care easy to access.

All Valley Urgent Care is a one-stop, affordable practice that exceeds patient health care needs. It’s a comprehensive health care solution for adult and pediatric patients.

The practice provides COVID-19 testing and treatment, on-site X-rays, lab services, and state-of-the-art tools to reach a diagnosis and develop a treatment plan as quickly as possible during a single visit. All Valley Urgent Care also works with home health agencies and rehab facilities.

The practice treats allergies, asthma, sore throats, migraines, nausea, upper respiratory infections, sexually transmitted diseases (STDs), urinary tract infections (UTIs), lacerations, sports injuries, and chronic diseases.

All Valley Urgent Care provides Department of Transportation (DOT) physicals, sports and school physicals, employment physicals, immigration physicals, workers’ compensation care, sick visits, wellness checkups, and much more.

The exceptional, respectful physicians see patients as quickly as possible but never rush medical care. They take the time to listen, thoroughly explain, and answer patient questions. All Valley Urgent Care uses the latest medical technologies and treatments to ensure superior health outcomes.

All Valley Urgent Care warmly welcomes new and existing patients and looks forward to helping them feel better faster.

To schedule an appointment at All Valley Urgent Care, call the office or request an appointment online today. You can also walk in without an appointment. The practice accepts insurance and those without for a reasonable cost.
