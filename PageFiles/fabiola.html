About Fabiola Tyson, NP-BC

Emergency Medicine & Urgent Care & Family Care in El Centro, Brawley, Calimesa and Calexico, CA

Fabiola Tyson, NP-BC


Fabiola Tyson, RN, BSN, is a registered nurse bringing more than five years of experience to All Valley Urgent Care, serving patients in Brawley and El Centro, California.

Fabiola knew she wanted to pursue a career in medicine from the time she was young. After high school, she enrolled at Imperial Valley College in Imperial, California, earning two associate’s degrees –– one in general science and the other in registered nursing.

Then, she enrolled at San Diego State University in California, earning her bachelor’s degree in registered nursing and graduating with honors. Before joining the team at All Valley Urgent Care, Fabiola obtained her Master of Science as a family nurse practitioner from the University of Southern California (USC) in Los Angeles.

All Valley Urgent Care is a bilingual practice, and Fabiola speaks English and Spanish. She has a friendly and compassionate demeanor and loves getting to know and educating the patients in her care.

Fabiola is passionate about learning. She regularly participates in continuing education and holds certifications in basic life support (BSL) and advanced cardiovascular life support (ACLS).

What’s more, she proudly holds membership in several professional organizations, including the American Nurses Association, the California Association of Nurse Practitioners, and the American Association of Critical Care Nurses.

Fabiola welcomes any individual or family in need of complete medical care to schedule an appointment at All Valley Urgent Care today.
