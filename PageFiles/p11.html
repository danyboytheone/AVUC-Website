Priscilla NP

Emergency Medicine & Urgent Care & Family Care in El Centro, Brawley, Calimesa and Calexico, CA

Priscilla


Priscilla Gutierrez, MSN, APRN, AGPCNP-BC, ACCNS, PHN is a compassionate and dedicated Nurse Practitioner at All Valley Urgent Care (AVUC) in Brawley, California. With a deep commitment to providing high-quality, patient-centered care, Priscilla utilizes her extensive clinical experience to assess, diagnose, and develop individualized care plans for a wide range of patients.

Priscilla holds a Master’s degree in Nursing from San Diego State University with a focus on Advanced Practice in Adult and Elderly Acute Critical Care Nurse Specialist, as well as board certification as an Adult-Gerontology Primary Care Nurse Practitioner. Her diverse background includes both acute care and outpatient care experience, which equips her to manage complex health concerns while offering compassionate care in the urgent care setting. At AVUC, Priscilla excels in performing thorough histories and physical examinations to create personalized treatment plans. She is skilled in a wide variety of procedures, including I&Ds, suturing, and administering care for common urgent conditions. Her role also includes specializing in workers' compensation, providing high-quality care for work-related injuries, and ensuring compliance with workers’ compensation regulations. She is passionate about helping patients navigate the recovery process and facilitating safe return-to-work plans.

In addition to her expertise in urgent care, Priscilla is experienced in conducting Department of Transportation (DOT) physicals and pre-employment physicals, ensuring that individuals meet the required medical standards for their respective jobs. She provides thorough evaluations, offering both clear documentation and patient education to ensure that patients are fit for their roles and maintain optimal health. Known for her compassionate and approachable demeanor, Priscilla creates a welcoming environment for all patients, ensuring they feel heard and valued. As a bilingual provider, she is able to effectively communicate with a diverse population, breaking down language barriers and ensuring quality care for all. Those in need of medical care are encouraged to schedule an appointment at All Valley Urgent Care in Brawley today.
